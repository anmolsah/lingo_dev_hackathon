# Anemia Mukt Bharat

## Overview

Anemia Mukt Bharat (AMB) is a national program aimed at reducing the prevalence of anemia across all age groups in India through a comprehensive strategy.

## Eligibility

- All citizens, with focus on:
- Children (6 months to 5 years)
- Adolescents (10-19 years)
- Pregnant and lactating women
- Women of reproductive age (15-49 years)

## Benefits

- Free Iron and Folic Acid (IFA) supplementation
- Nutritional counseling and education
- Deworming tablets
- Testing and screening for anemia
- Dietary diversification support
- Fortified foods distribution

## How to Access

1. Visit nearest Anganwadi Center or Health Sub-Center
2. Register for the program
3. Receive free IFA supplements
4. Attend nutrition counseling sessions
5. Get regular hemoglobin testing

## Program Components

- Six-fold strategy (6x6x6)
- Intensified Year-Round Strategy (IYRS)
- Community mobilization
- Behavior change communication
