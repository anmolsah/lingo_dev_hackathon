# PM-JANMAN Scheme

## Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Janjati Adivasi Nyaya Maha Abhiyan (PM-JANMAN) is a comprehensive initiative launched by the Government of India for the welfare of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs).

## Eligibility

- Members of recognized Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs)
- Residing in designated tribal areas
- Indian citizens with valid identification

## Benefits

- Housing assistance and construction support
- Clean drinking water facilities
- Electricity connections
- Road connectivity to remote areas
- Education support and scholarships
- Healthcare facilities and mobile medical units
- Livelihood support programs

## How to Apply

1. Visit your local tribal welfare office
2. Submit proof of PVTG membership
3. Fill out the application form
4. Attach required documents (Aadhaar, residence proof)
5. Track application status online through the portal
