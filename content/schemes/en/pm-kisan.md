# PM-KISAN Scheme

## Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a central sector scheme that provides income support to all landholding farmers' families across the country.

## Eligibility

- Small and marginal farmers
- Landholding farmers (individual or family)
- Valid land ownership records
- Active bank account linked to Aadhaar

## How to Apply

1. Visit the PM-KISAN portal (pmkisan.gov.in)
2. Register with Aadhaar number
3. Provide land ownership details
4. Link bank account
5. Submit application online or through Common Service Centers

## Required Documents

- Aadhaar card
- Bank account details
- Land ownership documents
- Mobile number
